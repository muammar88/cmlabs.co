    About Sequence

    Introduction

    A deep commitment to bringing digital solutions and products to the business ecosystem

    Test staging Sequence, under PT Statistik Hasil Pencarian, is a company under the cmlabs ecosystem that focuses on technical solution services and technology-based digital products. Sequence is here to be an end-to-end service that is integrated with each of our specialisations. Sequence has a mission to increase the company's business potential by leveraging the use of technology as the company's main line.

    Our Great Vision

    The endgame of Sequence is to become an end-to-end tech company that can be one of the technology-based companies that master the business line of digital products and solutions to support SEO digital marketing activities.

    The Blueprint of Sequence

    Sequence offers comprehensive digital solutions with a strong team of software engineering and product development experts.

    Our Specialities

    Product Operation

    With a focus on diverse sectors such as SEO, content writing, and media sentiment analysis, Sequence creates cutting-edge products such as STAT by Sequence, Media Monitoring, and SEO Tools. These products are designed to provide comprehensive solutions for various business needs, from search engine optimisation, online media analysis, assisting in technical activities, and other business activities.

    Project Operation

    As specialists in project development, Sequence has expertise in building and revamping websites. Our team is experienced in implementing the latest technologies to create an optimised user experience. Whether it's developing a new website or updating the look and functionality of an existing one, Sequence is committed to delivering high-quality results that meet the client's needs.

    Technical Consultancies and SEO Supports

    Sequence offers in-depth technical consulting services and SEO support. Our team of experts is ready to help you optimise website performance, improve page loading speed, and fix other technical issues. Our services include technical consulting, SEO technical support, website performance improvement, website migration, and infrastructure management.

    Quality Control and Maintenance

    Quality is our top priority. Our Quality Control and Maintenance Sequence teams ensure that all products and projects meet the highest standards. We conduct rigorous testing and provide ongoing maintenance support to ensure the smooth operation of your system.

    Research and Innovation

    Sequence is committed to continuous innovation and development of new technologies. Our research team actively seeks innovative solutions to complex business challenges. With a focus on developing the latest technologies, Sequence is always at the forefront of bringing relevant and effective solutions to our clients.
