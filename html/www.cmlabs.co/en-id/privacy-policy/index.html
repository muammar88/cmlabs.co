This policy discloses information collection and usage practices of PT CMLABS INDONESIA DIGITAL (referred to as "cmlabs", “we”, "us", or "our"), including on: https://cmlabs.co ("cmlabs website") and https://tools.cmlabs.co (“cmlabs SEO Tools").

We respect and protect the privacy of you and/or your company. We also aim to be as transparent as possible about how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information.

Hence, this Privacy Policy elucidates the types of information collected by our company, the purpose for which it is collected, and the measures we implement to ensure the transparency and security of such data. It serves to inform you of your rights as a user and our obligations as a data controller in compliance with applicable legal frameworks governing privacy and data protection.

We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of data security. To this end, we employ robust technological and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. We periodically review and update our security practices to address emerging threats and vulnerabilities.

Below is an enumeration of the specific instances where data is collected:

  • cmlabs Account Login & Registration: Data collected during user account creation and login to verify identity and provide account access.
  • cmlabs Web Analyzer: Information gathered to analyze website performance and provide insights.
  • cmlabs Traffic Projection Calculator: Data collected to estimate and project website traffic.
  • cmlabs Services Stat: Statistics and usage data gathered to monitor and improve service offerings.
  • Services Page: User interactions and preferences are collected on various service-specific pages.
  • Contact Page: Information submitted by users via contact forms for communication and inquiry responses.
  • Marketing Page: Data gathered to tailor and enhance marketing efforts based on user interaction.
  • User Activity on Website: Comprehensive logging of user activities to improve website functionality and user engagement.
  • Client Case Study: Confidential client data is shared only with explicit client approval, ensuring client privacy and compliance with confidentiality agreements.
  • Client Testimony: We conduct interviews with clients either online or offline, collecting data for publication on our website. Clients are given the option to consent or decline publication.
  • Contact Marketing Live Chat: Data collected through live chat for real-time marketing and support assistance.
  • cmlabs News: For the comment feature on our news portal, we disclose the nature of the data collected and the rationale behind enabling such features to facilitate interaction and feedback.
  • cmlabs Surge: Gather search volume, interest over time, and data interest by subregion for tracking trend developments from ongoing events, classified by event categories for current insights.
  • cmlabs Digital Media Buying: Domain lists from various online publishers and vendors, alongside user transaction histories for backlink service bookings on our platform.
  • cmlabs Career: Applicant data is collected to streamline candidate management for our HR team.
  • Whistleblower Protection: Alows users to confidentially report misconduct within cmlabs via a form, ensuring anonymity for those who prefer not to disclose their identity.
  • Partnership Proposal: Personal or corporate data from entities interested in partnership opportunities through a designated proposal form.

In all instances, cmlabs is committed to ensuring that data collection is conducted with the utmost respect for privacy and in compliance with applicable legal standards. We prioritize transparency and are diligent in obtaining necessary permissions, particularly in sensitive matters such as client data sharing and publication of testimonials.

Disclosures

cmlabs is committed to maintaining the integrity of user data in accordance with strict data protection standards. We expressly prohibit the sale or transfer of user data to third parties, except as explicitly authorized within predefined use cases. Additionally, the use or transfer of user data by cmlabs is confined solely to the specific and singular purpose of the services requested by the user, ensuring no deviation from this authorized use. Furthermore, it is our policy that user data shall not be utilized or transferred for the purposes of assessing creditworthiness or conducting any lending operations.
