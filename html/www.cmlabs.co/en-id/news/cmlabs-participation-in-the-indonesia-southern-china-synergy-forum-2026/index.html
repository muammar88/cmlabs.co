cmlabs reaffirmed its role as Indonesia’s digital representative on the global stage through its active participation in the Indonesia–Southern China Synergy Forum 2026, held on January 27, 2026, at the Shangri-La Shenzhen hotel, China.

In general, the forum brought together industry players, key stakeholders, and government representatives from Indonesia and Southern China to strengthen cross-border collaboration in economic development, technology, and talent development.

The forum was organized by PPIG and HSBE, and was attended by representatives of the Government of Indonesia through the Indonesian Consultant for China.

Explore our strategic role, the series of collaborations established, and the long-term contributions delivered for Indonesia and the global market through this forum in the full review below.

About the Indonesia–Southern China Synergy Forum 2026

The Indonesia–Southern China Synergy Forum 2026 is a strategic bilateral forum held on January 27, 2026, at the Shangri-La Shenzhen, Southern China, with the objective of strengthening cooperation between Indonesia and the Southern China region across various economic and development sectors.

According to the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Indonesia Portal, a total of 12 cooperation documents were signed, covering a wide range of sectors, including agriculture, healthcare, digital payment systems, and digital infrastructure, with a total value of USD 100 million.

The forum was designed as a platform that brought together business actors, strategic stakeholders, and government representatives from both countries to explore concrete investment and collaboration opportunities, particularly in agriculture, healthcare, and other sectors relevant to current global dynamics.

The event was initiated by the Consulate General of the Republic of Indonesia in Guangzhou and received support from various parties, including bilateral business organizations and Indonesian government institutions engaged in economic diplomacy and investment promotion.

The forum also served as an important convergence point for strengthening business networks and economic diplomacy through cross-sector dialogue, opening opportunities for Indonesian business actors to expand partnership networks with companies and investors from Southern China.

cmlabs Role at the Indonesia–Southern China Synergy Forum 2026

As an integrated digital marketing service provider from Indonesia, cmlabs participated as a strategic partner, bridging Chinese technology with the needs and market opportunities in Indonesia.

At the forum, cmlabs contributed as an Indonesian digital marketing company that actively drives global expansion through discussions on overseas market expansion strategies and business growth in Indonesia, particularly following the establishment of cooperation between cmlabs and Kingdee, a leading enterprise software solutions developer from China.

This commitment was further strengthened through the signing of a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) between cmlabs and Kingdee, serving as a strategic foundation for collaboration in supporting market expansion and the development of Kingdee’s ERP business in the Indonesian market.

The collaboration between cmlabs and Kingdee also highlights cmlabs’ role in discussions on market entry approaches, positioning enhancement, and the adaptation of digital strategies aligned with the unique characteristics of the Indonesian market.

This further reinforces cmlabs’ position as a strategic overseas market enabler, a partner capable of bridging international technology providers with Indonesian companies, while supporting sustainable global market expansion through localized adaptation strategies.

Together with Kingdee, cmlabs Is Opening the Gateway to the Indonesia–China Market

Through this partnership, cmlabs supports Kingdee by developing international marketing strategies, sharpening market positioning for entry into the Indonesian market, providing technical and development support, and delivering deep insights into the local ecosystem, including market behavior, enterprise needs, and data-driven digital activation.

This collaboration opens new opportunities for businesses in Indonesia to access world-class technology solutions. For cmlabs, the partnership reflects our role as a bridge between global technology providers and Indonesian companies undergoing digital transformation.

Developing Digital Talent Through University Collaboration

Beyond the business aspect, cmlabs is also committed to nurturing Indonesia’s future leaders by developing student talent through official collaborations with several of our partner universities.

This initiative aims to create tangible opportunities for Indonesia’s younger generation while accelerating knowledge transfer in enterprise systems, digital marketing, and global business.

Diplomatic Dialogue for Strengthening Indonesia’s Brand in the Global Market

As part of the forum series, the leadership team of cmlabs also held a diplomatic meeting with Djauhari Oratmangun, Ambassador of the Republic of Indonesia to China and Mongolia.

The discussion explored opportunities to strengthen both personal and corporate branding of Indonesia in international markets, as well as cmlabs’ role across every stage of international collaboration, as a strategic partner connecting Indonesia’s digitalization potential in a more mature, competitive, and globally ready approach.

cmlabs’ Vision and Objectives Moving Forward

Through our official participation in this forum, PT cmlabs Indonesia Digital reaffirms its commitment to continuously serving as a strategic bridge for international collaboration and digital innovation.

We are determined to bring Indonesia’s capabilities onto the global stage by connecting technology, ideas, and opportunities that deliver tangible impact for businesses and local talent alike.

Looking ahead, cmlabs will continue to grow as a full-spectrum digital marketing service provider, encompassing strategy, marketing, creative, technology, data, and performance, ensuring that every solution delivered is not only relevant but also transformative for business clients and the broader Indonesian ecosystem.

We see the future as a space to connect, build, and empower, connecting market needs with technology, developing talent and ecosystems, and driving digital innovations that generate positive impact for Indonesia’s industries.

With this vision, cmlabs is committed to becoming a trusted global partner, capable of activating digital ecosystems on the international stage, while serving as a source of inspiration for Indonesia’s creative generation through impactful and wide-reaching collaborations.
