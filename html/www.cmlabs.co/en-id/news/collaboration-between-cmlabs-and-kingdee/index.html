cmlabs officially conducted a corporate visit to Kingdee, one of the largest Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) providers in China, as an initial step toward a strategic collaboration aimed at developing and expanding ERP solutions in the Indonesian market.

This visit marked the opening phase of partnership discussions between Kingdee and cmlabs, with cmlabs positioned as Kingdee’s official partner in Indonesia. The collaboration focuses on digital marketing initiatives, as well as the management and development of ERP products and services tailored to the Indonesian market.

Learn more about the potential of this collaboration and how cmlabs plays a strategic role in bridging global ERP technology with the specific needs of businesses in Indonesia.

Getting to Know Kingdee

Kingdee is a global technology company based in China, specializing in cloud- and AI-based Enterprise Management and Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) solutions. Kingdee delivers cross-domain business management solutions designed to support more agile, integrated, and sustainable enterprise growth.

Through its service portfolio, Kingdee provides integrated solutions covering finance and accounting, human resources, supply chain, manufacturing, sales, and operational management. These solutions are designed to support businesses of all sizes, from small and medium enterprises (SMEs) to large-scale enterprises.

With a cloud-native approach and enterprise management powered by AI, Kingdee’s solutions enable companies to manage data in real time, improve operational efficiency, enhance transparency, and support data-driven decision-making across various business functions and industries.

Backed by decades of experience and a global user base across various industry sectors, Kingdee has become one of the key players in enterprise digital transformation in Asia, particularly in China.

cmlabs Visits Kingdee Headquarters in Shenzhen, China

Driven by Kingdee’s interest in cmlabs’ capabilities and experience within Indonesia’s digital ecosystem, cmlabs received an official invitation to visit Kingdee’s headquarters and Kingdee Software Park. This visit marked the initial phase of a strategic Indonesia–China collaboration, particularly in the development and marketing of ERP solutions within Indonesia’s digital ecosystem.

On January 26, 2026, cmlabs conducted a visit to Kingdee’s headquarters in Shenzhen, China, to engage in direct discussions on potential collaboration opportunities. The discussions focused on exploring the ERP platform’s potential, including its management and development for the Indonesian market, aligned with national-scale business needs, local government regulations, as well as the exploration of CSR and academic collaboration opportunities with Indonesian universities through cmlabs.

cmlabs as the Official ERP Platform Marketing Partner in Indonesia

In this collaboration, cmlabs serves as a strategic partner in the marketing and development of Kingdee’s ERP platform in Indonesia.

Going forward, the partnership will leverage the full scope of cmlabs’ services, ranging from digital marketing strategy planning and execution, visibility optimization through SEO services, to technical optimization, including ERP platform development directly supported by Kingdee.

Through this integrated approach, cmlabs supports Kingdee in building strong digital visibility in Indonesia, enhancing local market education around ERP solutions, and ensuring an optimal platform experience that is relevant to local business needs and regulatory requirements.

ERP Localization Process Aligned with Indonesian Regulations and Business Needs

As a trusted digital marketing company in Indonesia, cmlabs understands that the successful adoption of technology by Indonesian enterprises is not determined solely by system sophistication. It is equally influenced by how well a solution aligns with government regulations, market conditions, and local business needs.

Therefore, within this strategic collaboration, ERP localization has become one of the key focus areas to ensure that the solutions developed are not only technologically advanced, but also compliant with Indonesian regulations and standards.

Through this partnership, Kingdee’s ERP platform will be adapted to meet various local requirements, including taxation and financial reporting regulations, applicable accounting standards, and common operational practices in Indonesia. This approach aims to deliver ERP solutions that are relevant, secure, and ready for implementation across multiple industries in Indonesia.

Beyond regulatory considerations, the localization process will also take into account Indonesia’s business dynamics and varying levels of digital readiness. This ensures that the ERP solutions developed can sustainably support operational efficiency, data transparency, and data-driven decision-making.

Exploring CSR Opportunities and Academic Collaboration with Indonesian Universities

During the meeting, cmlabs and Kingdee also explored opportunities for corporate social responsibility (CSR) initiatives and academic collaborations with leading universities in Indonesia.

These initiatives aim to ensure that the solutions developed contribute to digital talent development while strengthening Indonesia’s technology and education ecosystem.

In this collaboration, cmlabs will act as a bridging partner, focusing on the development of industry-relevant programs such as education and training for young talents to gain exposure to ERP systems and cloud- and AI-based enterprise management technologies, as well as support for research activities and practice-based learning initiatives, including internship programs.

cmlabs’ Vision and Objectives Moving Forward

Through this strategic collaboration, cmlabs recognizes the importance of injecting global technology into Indonesia’s business ecosystem to accelerate a competitive digital transformation.

We believe that this collaboration with Kingdee represents an important first step toward delivering world-class business technology solutions that are relevant, localized, and ready to address the evolving needs of businesses in Indonesia.

Looking ahead, cmlabs will continue to position itself as a bridge and access provider between global technologies and Indonesian businesses, across solution development, digital marketing, and other strategic initiatives, while ensuring full regulatory compliance and alignment with industry needs and local business dynamics.
