Technology Lookup

Published at Oct 13, 2023

What is Technology Lookup?

Technology Lookup is a free tool from cmlabs that you can use to identify what technology was used to build a website. This tool allows you to view the operating system, web server, widgets, javascript libraries, and find the full version of the technology used on a site.

You can use this tool for free. To use Technology Lookup is also quite easy. You just need to enter a URL and our robot will help you find the technology used on that URL.

Why Do You Need Lookup Technology?

You are not the only one who wants to know the technology on a website. Here are the reasons that might be behind someone wanting to know what is used to build a website:

  • Curiosity - some people find the technology of a website out of curiosity and want to learn about the technology used in developing a website.

  • Website Monitoring - others monitor the technology of a website with the aim of taking security or surveillance measures in order to improve the technology used.

  • Market Research - You may be doing market research because you want to know the technology used by your competitors.

  • Monitor Technology changes - Monitor changes in competitor technologies such as new CMS, e-commerce platforms or other technologies.

The Benefits of Technology Lookup

In addition, Technology Lookup also provides several benefits for you. Starting from benefits for personal websites or to monitoring competitor websites. Check out the following explanation:

Identify Technology On Your Website

You can use this tool to do research for your business needs. For example, you need to identify the technology used on your website and make some technology upgrades if necessary.

This way, you can provide better performance and user experience on your web pages. This will also have a good effect on business success, such as bringing in traffic to generate potential leads.

Technology Analysis on Competitor Websites

This tool can also help you analyze the technology used by your competitors. This will later give you an idea of the advantages of your competitors, what technology they use, and what kind of technology is most appropriate for your business.

Thus the discussion of what Technology Lookup is. You have nothing to lose using this tool. The reason is that the method of use is quite easy, can be accessed for free, and of course, can reveal the technology of a website as a whole. Explore website technology now!

How to Use Technology Lookup cmlabs

The following guide was created to assist you in using this tool. Follow these steps:

Go to Technology Lookup Page

Please open the Technology Lookup page from cmlabs first. On that page, you can add the URL you want to see the technology for, identify the technology on the website being tracked, and view the history of Technology Lookup usage.

Figure 1: View of the Technology Lookup tool page from cmlabs

Enter Website URL

In order to identify the technology on a website, you must enter the website URL in the field provided. Make sure you have entered the URL in the correct format (e.g https://example.com). Then, click the "LOOKUP URL" button to give the command to this tool to start checking the technology on the website.

Figure 2: The display when the user has entered the website URL in the provided field.

Identify Technology Stacks on Websites

After a while, you can see the stack of technology used on the website in the Technology section. The Technology Lookup tool allows you to view widgets, operating systems, javascript libraries, web servers, and identify the full version of the technology a site uses.

Figure 3: Display of the results of monitoring the technology stack used on the tracked website.

Monitor Technology Lookup Usage History

Finally, you can also view a list of Technology Lookup usage history. In the list, you will see a list of website URLs that you have monitored through the Technology Lookup from cmlabs.

Figure 4: A list of history using technology lookup from cmlabs.

Login After 5 Times Usage

Some cmlabs free tools require a login process after 5 times use, Technology Lookup is one of them. If you have used it 5 times, our system will automatically display a message stating that you have reached the usage limit.

Figure 5: Usage warning has reached the limit. In this warning, there is a login button that directs to the login page.

You can log in by clicking the login button on the warning message that appears. Next, you will be directed to the cmlabs login page. Please enter the required data, or you can log in via Google.

Figure 6: cmlabs login page

Once you have successfully logged in, you will be able to return to using this tool for free checking. By logging in, the system can save historical data on the use of this tool in the last one year.
